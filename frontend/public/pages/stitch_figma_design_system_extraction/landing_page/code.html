Common Questions

Is MedTech Pro really free for patients? expand_more

Yes! Our core medication tracking and plant journey features are 100% free for patients forever. We believe health management should be accessible to everyone.

How are caregivers vetted? expand_more

Every caregiver on our platform undergoes a rigorous 4-step verification process, including background checks, certification verification, and personal interviews.

Can my doctor see my progress? expand_more

Absolutely. You can choose to share your adherence reports directly with your healthcare provider via email or a secure link through the app.

What happens if I miss a dose? expand_more

The app will send gentle reminders. If the dose isn't marked as taken within your set grace period, your designated family members or caregivers will receive a notification.
